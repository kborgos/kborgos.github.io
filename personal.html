September 2017: Moved from Mayaguez, PR to Evanston, IL to pursue my PhD at Northwestern University!

I was born and raised in the small, but beautiful island of Puerto Rico. I moved out in 2017 to pursue my graduate education in the United States.

As a child, one of the first objects I held was a gaming console controller. Unfortunately, I don't have as many opportunities to play video games these days, but I still like to keep up with the community and game once in a while!

Additionally, one of my lifelong passions has been art. I enjoy drawing during my spare time because it allows me to get creative and relieve stress. I hope to share some of my creations here in the future!

Finally, I like to explore new places and go on adventures with friends and family. I made my first international trip in the summer of 2018 to visit South Korea. My goal is to slowly add many more countries to my list!
