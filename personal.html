Personal

  • I was born and raised in the small, but beautiful island of Puerto Rico (Snow? What is that?). I moved out in 2017 to pursue my graduate education in the Northern side of the United States.
  • As a child, one of the first phrases I learned was "please pass me the controller". Unfortunately, I don't have as many opportunities to play video games these days, but I still like to keep up with the community and game once in a while.
  • Additionally, one of my lifelong passions has been art. I enjoy drawing during my spare time because it allows me to get creative and relieve stress. I hope to share some of my creations here in the future!
  • Finally, I enjoy to explore new places and go on adventures with my loved ones. I'm hoping I can make Asia my next destination in the near future!
