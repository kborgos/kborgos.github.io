About Me

My name is Katya Borgos-Rodriguez and I am an undergraduate student from the University of Puerto Rico at Mayaguez majoring in Computer Engineering and specializing in Computing Systems (Software). I am expecting to graduate in May of 2017 and plan to pursue a Ph.D in Human-Computer Interaction immediately after. When I am not studying or programming, I will most likely be drawing or playing video games. Drawing has been one of my passions ever since I was little, which is why I feel very fortunate to have been chosen to work in the Sketch Recognition Lab in Texas A&M University!

If you have any questions, please feel free to reach out to me via email at katya.borgos@upr.edu.

This experience was made possible by DREU, a joint project of the CRA-W and the Coalision to Diversity Computing, and the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Texas A&M University.
